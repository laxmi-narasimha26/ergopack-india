Which System Is Right for Your Operation?

Choose X-pert Line If:

  • Shipment failure would result in catastrophic consequences
  • Your operation requires regulatory compliance verification
  • You ship high-value pharmaceuticals, electronics, or automotive components
  • You need data-driven proof of load integrity
  • Precision and verifiable control are non-negotiable

Choose E-conomy Line If:

  • You require reliable, proven performance
  • Standard load security meets your operational needs
  • You value German engineering quality
  • You want the trusted foundation of the ErgoPack system
  • Consistent operation is your priority
