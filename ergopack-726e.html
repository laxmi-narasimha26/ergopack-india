ErgoPack 726ELIGHT-DUTY

Mobile and Ergonomic Pallet Strapping System for Light-Duty Applications

Professional Light-Duty Strapping Solution

The ErgoPack 726E is specifically engineered for light-duty packaging applications, offering optimal performance with narrower straps and controlled tension for delicate products and lighter loads.

Featuring 400-2500N tension power and 13-16mm strap width, the 726E delivers cost-effective strapping for applications that don't require heavy-duty capabilities, while maintaining ErgoPack's renowned ergonomic design and reliability.

Light-Duty Advantages

  • Optimized for lighter loads and delicate products
  • Narrower straps (13-16mm) reduce material costs
  • Controlled tension (400-2500N) prevents product damage
  • Cost-effective solution for light-duty operations
  • Same ergonomic design as heavy-duty models
